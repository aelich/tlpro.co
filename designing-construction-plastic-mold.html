DESIGN & CONSTRUCTION OF INJECTION MOLDING SYSTEM

Our 30 years experience in the design and manufacturing of injection molding systems is at your disposal. We have produced thousands of molds so far.

We are capable of producing molds and metal parts in a very short time period. We continue to develop our injection molding systems and to provide quick-turn machining to global facilities in Europe. Our industrial 3D printing services create an easier path for product developers, designers and engineers to move from early prototyping to low-volume production.

TILE LOCK is one of the fastest growing manufacturers of prototypes and low-volume production parts with manufacturing facilities in several countries. Our company continuously strives to accelerate and inprove manufacturing process with persistent research and development.

Our Process in Creating New Molds:

  • Design and performance analysis.
  • Prototype production.
  • Series production and mold testing prior to mass production.
  • Packaging and shiping.
